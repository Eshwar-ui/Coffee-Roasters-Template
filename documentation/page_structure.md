# 📄 Page Structure

A detailed breakdown of the pages included in this template and their intended use.

- **[index.html](file:///c:/Users/Eswar/Desktop/html%20templates/Coffee%20Roasters%20Template/coffee-roaster-template/index.html)**: The primary homepage. Features include a hero slider, press logos, a "how it works" section, and a product showcase.
- **[pages/index-2.html](file:///c:/Users/Eswar/Desktop/html%20templates/Coffee%20Roasters%20Template/coffee-roaster-template/pages/index-2.html)**: An alternative homepage layout with a modern video background and different storytelling approach.
- **[pages/about.html](file:///c:/Users/Eswar/Desktop/html%20templates/Coffee%20Roasters%20Template/coffee-roaster-template/pages/about.html)**: Dedicated to company history, values, and team members. Includes a "Core Values" section with interactive icons.

## Shop & Product Flow
- **[pages/shop.html](file:///c:/Users/Eswar/Desktop/html%20templates/Coffee%20Roasters%20Template/coffee-roaster-template/pages/shop.html)**: Full product catalog. Includes a sidebar for filtering (Category, Region, Roast Level) and sorting options.
- **[pages/product-single.html](file:///c:/Users/Eswar/Desktop/html%20templates/Coffee%20Roasters%20Template/coffee-roaster-template/pages/product-single.html)**: Detailed view for a single coffee product, featuring high-res imagery, flavor notes, and add-to-cart functionality.
- **[pages/cart.html](file:///c:/Users/Eswar/Desktop/html%20templates/Coffee%20Roasters%20Template/coffee-roaster-template/pages/cart.html)**: A slide-over or full-page summary of items selected for purchase.

## Communication & Account
- **[pages/contact.html](file:///c:/Users/Eswar/Desktop/html%20templates/Coffee%20Roasters%20Template/coffee-roaster-template/pages/contact.html)**: Contact form (ready for Formspree), multi-location map, and direct contact details.
- **[pages/dashboard-user.html](file:///c:/Users/Eswar/Desktop/html%20templates/Coffee%20Roasters%20Template/coffee-roaster-template/pages/dashboard-user.html)**: A comprehensive user portal to manage orders, subscriptions, addresses, and account messages.
- **[pages/dashboard-admin.html](file:///c:/Users/Eswar/Desktop/html%20templates/Coffee%20Roasters%20Template/coffee-roaster-template/pages/dashboard-admin.html)**: A specialized admin UI shell for managing inventory, viewing global sales analytics, and replying to customer messages.
